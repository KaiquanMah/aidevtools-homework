# Finnish Language Curriculum (Levels 0 - A2)

This curriculum is designed for English speakers learning Finnish, based on CEFR standards.

## Level 0: The Absolute Basics
**Goal**: Familiarization with sounds, alphabet, and very basic survival phrases.

### Lesson 1: Alphabet & Pronunciation
- **Topics**: The Finnish Alphabet, Vowels & Diphthongs, Double Consonants (kk, pp, tt).
- **Grammar**: Vowel Harmony introduction (a/o/u vs ä/ö/y).
- **Vocabulary**: Basic words (kissa, koira, talo, bussi).

### Lesson 2: Greetings & Manners
- **Topics**: Saying Hello/Goodbye, Thank you, Yes/No.
- **Grammar**: N/A (Fixed phrases).
- **Vocabulary**: Hei, Moi, Huomenta, Kiitos, Kyllä, Ei, Anteeksi.

### Lesson 3: Numbers (0-10) & Colors
- **Topics**: Counting to 10, recognizing colors.
- **Grammar**: N/A.
- **Vocabulary**: nolla-kymmenen, sininen, punainen, vihreä.

## Level A1: Beginner
**Goal**: Communicate in simple everyday situations. Understand basic sentences.

### Lesson 4: Introductions & Personal Info
- **Topics**: Asking "What is your name?", "Where are you from?".
- **Grammar**: Verb *olla* (to be) - Minä olen, Sinä olet. Question words: *Mikä*, *Kuka*.
- **Vocabulary**: Nimi, koti, maa (country), kieli (language).

### Lesson 5: My Life (Family & Home)
- **Topics**: Describing family members and where you live.
- **Grammar**: Possessive structure "Minulla on" (I have). Genitive case (Minun...).
- **Vocabulary**: äiti, isä, sisko, veli, talo, kerrostalo.

### Lesson 6: Days, Weather & Numbers (11-100)
- **Topics**: Days of the week, simple weather description.
- **Grammar**: *Partitive* case introduction (numbers > 1 + partitive).
- **Vocabulary**: Maanantai-Sunnuntai, aurinko paistaa, sataa.

### Lesson 7: Food & Shopping (Partitive Basics)
- **Topics**: Buying food, ordering coffee.
- **Grammar**: Partitive case with food/uncountable nouns (Haluaisin kahvia).
- **Vocabulary**: kahvi, maito, leipä, kauppa, hinta.

### Lesson 8: Basic Questions & Location
- **Topics**: Asking where things are.
- **Grammar**: Location cases: Missä (In), Mistä (From), Mihin (To).
- **Vocabulary**: Suomi, Englanti, koulu, työ, kaupunki.

## Level A2: Basic User (Elementary)
**Goal**: Describe past events, routine tasks, and handle service situations.

### Lesson 9: Daily Routine & Basic Verbs
- **Topics**: Describing a normal day.
- **Grammar**: Verb Types 1-5 conjugation in Present Tense. Consonant gradation (kpt).
- **Vocabulary**: herätä, syödä, mennä, tulla, nukkua.

### Lesson 10: Travel & Directions
- **Topics**: Taking the bus/train, asking for directions.
- **Grammar**: Imperative (Orders/Directions).
- **Vocabulary**: oikea/vasen, asema, lippu, juna, bussi.

### Lesson 11: Past Events (The Imperfect)
- **Topics**: Telling what you did yesterday.
- **Grammar**: Imperfect tense (*i*-marker). "Olin", "Kävin".
- **Vocabulary**: eilen, viime viikolla, aamulla.

### Lesson 12: Work & Professions
- **Topics**: Talking about jobs and workplaces.
- **Grammar**: "Työskennellä" + missä (working at...). ESSIVE case (Olen opettajana - as a teacher).
- **Vocabulary**: lääkäri, opettaja, insinööri, toimisto.

### Lesson 13: Making Requests & Polite Speech
- **Topics**: "I would like...".
- **Grammar**: Conditional mood (-isi-). "Haluaisin".
- **Vocabulary**: Voinko, saisinko.
